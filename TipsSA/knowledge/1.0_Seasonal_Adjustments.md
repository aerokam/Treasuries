adjusts# 1.0 Seasonal Adjustments (SA)

## Overview
Treasury Inflation-Protected Securities (TIPS) yields are calculated based on the "Clean Price", which is Canty's term for the unadjusted price; this price does not include the inflation adjustment component. However, since TIPS are indexed to the Non-Seasonally Adjusted CPI (CPI-NSA), their monthly performance is subject to predictable seasonal patterns (e.g., higher inflation in spring, lower in autumn).

The **Seasonal Adjustment (SA)** adjusts these yields by applying SA factors, providing a more accurate comparison of real yields across different months of the year by adjusting the yields to be consistent with institutional traders' views of seasonal impacts on nominal returns.

## Terminology
- **SA (Seasonal Adjustment)**: The multiplicative factor used to normalize for seasonal inflation patterns.
- **SA Yield (Seasonally Adjusted Yield)**: The real yield derived from a Seasonally Adjusted Price.

## The Transformation
The transformation converts a **Market Price** to a **Seasonally Adjusted Price**, which is then used to derive the **SA Yield**.

### 1. Price Adjustment Formula
\[ P_{SA} = P_{Price} \times \frac{S_{settle}}{S_{maturity}} \]

Where:
- \( P_{SA} \): Seasonally Adjusted Price.
- \( P_{Price} \): Market Price.
- \( S_{settle} \): SA Factor for the settlement date (\( \frac{RefCPI_{NSA}}{RefCPI_{SA}} \)).
- \( S_{maturity} \): SA Factor for the maturity date (\( \frac{RefCPI_{NSA}}{RefCPI_{SA}} \)).

Note: The SA Factor (\( S \)) is the ratio of the Reference CPI (Non-Seasonally Adjusted) to the Reference CPI (Seasonally Adjusted) for the specific date. This formula is generic with respect to the type of price. Currently, the system uses FedInvest and broker ask prices, but it is not locked to "ask" prices only.

### 2. Yield Derivation
The **SA Yield** is derived from \( P_{SA} \) using the standard bond yield-to-maturity (YTM) formula (Actual/Actual day count convention, semi-annual compounding).

## Data Sources
- **Prices**: Sourced from FedInvest (daily) or uploaded via Broker CSV (Schwab/Fidelity). Currently, these sources provide **Ask Prices**.
- **SA Factors**: Calculated monthly from BLS CPI-U data (NSA vs SA components) and hosted in the TIPS Portal R2 bucket.
