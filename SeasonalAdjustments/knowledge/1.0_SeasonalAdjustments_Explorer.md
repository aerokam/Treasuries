# 1.0 Seasonal Adjustments Explorer

## Purpose
A standalone educational app that walks through the mechanics of TIPS seasonal adjustment in three progressive tabs: the raw CPI seasonal cycle → the SA Factor derived from it → how that factor shifts TIPS prices and yields.

## Dependencies
- [YieldCurves/knowledge/1.0_Seasonal_Adjustments.md](../../YieldCurves/knowledge/1.0_Seasonal_Adjustments.md) — canonical SA math (P_SA = P × S(settle)/S(maturity))
- [knowledge/DATA_DICTIONARY.md](../../knowledge/DATA_DICTIONARY.md) — Ref CPI, SA Factor, dated date definitions

---

## Data Source

**R2 file**: `TIPS/RefCpiNsaSa.csv`  
Columns: `date, nsa, sa` (daily Ref CPI NSA and SA, produced by `shared/src/ref-cpi.js` App. B interpolation).  
The SA Factor for any date is computed on the fly: `factor = nsa / sa`.

**10-year window** (Tab 0, Tab 0 factor chart): rows from `${maxYear−11}-01-01` through the latest date in the file. This gives ≥10 full calendar years of month-over-month data.

**5-year average** (Tab 1, Tab 2): for each calendar day `mm-dd`, average the SA Factor across the 5 most recent complete years. Used for the annual curve and the interactive price-adjustment slider.

---

## Tab 0 — CPI Cycle

### Panel 1: MoM CPI-U bar chart
- X-axis: 12 calendar months (Jan–Dec)
- Each bar: average month-over-month % change in CPI-U NSA for that calendar month, across ~10 years
- Gray dots: individual year values, spread horizontally within the column (sorted oldest-left to newest-right, 70% of column width) so each year is individually hoverable; tooltip shows `YYYY: ±X.XX%`
- The CPI-U calendar month is the Ref CPI month minus 3 (the 3-month lag). For Ref CPI months Jan/Feb/Mar the CPI-U year is the prior calendar year (shift < 0 → year − 1).
- Green line overlay: same average computed on CPI-U SA — should be flat by construction
- Dashed horizontal lines: H1 (Jan–Jun) and H2 (Jul–Dec) averages

### Panel 2: SA Factor time series (last 10 years)
- X-axis: calendar time, anchored to **January 1 of the first year that has a January data point** in the series (not the raw first row date). This ensures the axis always starts at a January gridline regardless of when the CSV begins.
- Major gridlines + year label + angled "Jan" label at each January. Minor gridlines + angled label at Apr/Jul/Oct. Tick only for all other months.
- Data lines start at `chartStart` — rows before that date are excluded from the path.
- **Blue line**: daily SA Factor = Ref CPI NSA / Ref CPI SA
- **Amber dashed line**: monthly CPI-U NSA/SA ratio, plotted 3 months earlier than its Ref CPI month (to show where the underlying CPI-U actually was)

## Tab 1 — SA Factor (annual)

- The SA Factor S = Ref CPI NSA / Ref CPI SA folded into a single calendar year
- Each point is the 5-year average SA Factor for that month/day
- X-axis: Jan → Dec (day of year 0–364)
- Maturity month markers (Jan/Apr/Jul/Oct 15) shown as vertical lines + dots
- Shows trough Feb–Mar, peak Aug–Sep

## Tab 2 — Price Adjustment (interactive)

### Curve
Same 5-year-average annual SA Factor curve as Tab 1, plus:
- **Settlement marker** (cyan): vertical line + dot at the slider-selected day of year
- **Selected maturity marker** (amber, solid): vertical line + dot at the maturity month/day 15
- **Other maturity markers** (amber, faded): the other three TIPS issue months

### Seasonal Gap Triangle
Drawn between the settlement and maturity points; updates with every slider tick.

- **Vertex A**: settlement dot `(x(settleDoy), y(S(settle)))`
- **Vertex B**: maturity dot `(x(matDoy), y(S(maturity)))`
- **Vertex C** (right angle): `(x(matDoy), y(S(settle)))` — maturity x at settlement y
- **Horizontal leg** (dashed): A → C — the time axis of the stub, held at settlement's seasonal level
- **Vertical leg** (solid, 2.5px): C → B — the raw seasonal gap `S(settle) − S(maturity)`; this is the quantity the price adjustment removes
- **Fill**: semi-transparent, green (`--up`) when S(settle) < S(maturity) (SA yield rises), red (`--down`) when S(settle) > S(maturity) (SA yield falls)
- **Label** on the vertical leg: `S(settle)/S(maturity) − 1` as `±X.XXX%`
- Triangle is suppressed when `|S(settle) − S(maturity)| < 0.00005` (settlement on or very near maturity day)

### Readout panel
- S(settle), S(maturity), stub in days/months, price-adjustment ratio S(settle)/S(maturity), "capture" S(maturity)/S(settle) − 1
- Verdict text explaining direction and magnitude of SA yield shift

### Stub logic
`stub = matDoy − settleDoy; if stub < 0, stub += 365` — always the forward distance in days to the maturity month/day.

## Key Formulas

| Symbol | Definition |
|--------|-----------|
| S(t) | Ref CPI NSA(t) / Ref CPI SA(t) — SA Factor on day t |
| P_SA | P_market × S(settle) / S(maturity) — seasonally adjusted price |
| Ratio | S(settle) / S(maturity) — the price multiplier |
| Capture | S(maturity) / S(settle) − 1 — % impact on price |

## Architecture
- Single file: `SeasonalAdjustments/index.html` — no build step, pure ES module style inline script
- Data fetched from R2 at load; 6-second timeout; falls back to `buildDailyFromMonthly()` (monthly anchors interpolated to daily) if fetch fails
- `drawCpiChart()` — Tab 0 panel 1
- `drawFactorChart()` — Tab 0 panel 2
- `drawAnnualCurve()` — Tab 1
- `drawCurve(settleDoy)` — Tab 2 (called by `update()` on every slider input)
